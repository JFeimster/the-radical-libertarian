## Tags

Use lowercase hyphenated tags.

Examples:

- self-ownership
- decentralization
- free-speech
- digital-privacy
- surveillance-state
- fiat-money
- platform-power
- manufactured-consent
- exit-over-voice
- parallel-systems
- local-groups
- media-literacy

## Statuses

Use these consistently:

- `planned`
- `draft`
- `review`
- `active`
- `published`
- `archived`
- `needs-url`
- `needs-review`
- `external-resource`

## URL Rules

Use lowercase and hyphens.

Good:

- `central-bank-alchemy.html`
- `npc-narrative-checker.html`
- `old-podcast-episodes.html`

Bad:

- `CentralBankAlchemy.html`
- `NPC_Narrative_Checker.html`
- `Old Podcast Episodes.html`
