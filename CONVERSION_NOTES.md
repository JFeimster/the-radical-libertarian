# Conversion Notes

## Homepage Conversion Flow

Primary flow:

1. Visitor lands on homepage
2. Visitor chooses:
   - Manifesto
   - Newsletter
   - Explore
3. Secondary routing pushes into:
   - Essays
   - Podcast
   - Tools
   - Resources
   - Archive
   - Assets
