The Manifesto

The operating principles behind The Radical Libertarian: self-ownership, decentralization, free speech, exit over voice, and radical personal sovereignty.

Freedom is not a permission slip.

This project is for people who do not want their mind, money, speech, privacy, labor, or future managed by committee goblins with laminated badges.

01. Self-Ownership

You own your body. You own your mind. You own your time. Any entity claiming a percentage of these without consent is not your guardian. It is your landlord with better branding.

02. Exit Over Voice

Begging broken systems to become less broken is not a strategy. Build alternatives. Build leverage. Build parallel rails. Then leave the suggestion box to the people still emotionally invested in the cage.

03. Speech Is Not Violence

Bad ideas should be defeated in public, not disappeared by frightened hall monitors with moderation dashboards.

04. Decentralize Everything

Concentrated power attracts the exact personality type that should never have concentrated power. Fragment the throne. Then sell the pieces at a yard sale.

The Operating Rule

The Radical Libertarian exists to publish essays, podcast episodes, resources, and tools for people who would rather build sovereignty than complain professionally.
