Glossary

A glossary of liberty, money, privacy, decentralization, and media-literacy terms.

Self-Ownership

The principle that the individual owns their body, labor, mind, time, and conscience.

Core

Decentralization

The distribution of power, infrastructure, decision-making, and control away from single choke points.

Power

Agorism

A strategy focused on voluntary exchange, counter-economics, and building outside coercive systems.

Exit

Cypherpunk

A movement and ethos using cryptography and code to defend privacy, speech, and individual autonomy.

Tech

Fiat Money

Currency whose value is enforced by government decree and institutional acceptance rather than commodity backing.

Money
