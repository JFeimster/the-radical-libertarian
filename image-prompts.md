# Image Prompts

Prompts for creating visual assets for The Radical Libertarian.

## Global Style

Brutalist punk-libertarian editorial design. Cream paper background, black ink, red accents, thick black borders, hard offset shadows, torn paper texture, zine layout, high contrast, bold type energy, philosophical rebellion, no corporate polish.

Avoid:

- Official party logos
- Fake seals
- Fake badges
- Stock political clipart
- Generic patriotic wallpaper
- Overly clean SaaS gradients

## Homepage OG Image

Create a 1200x630 Open Graph image for a brutalist libertarian media site called The Radical Libertarian. Huge bold headline: “Freedom Is Not Supposed To Be Comfortable.” Cream paper background, black and red palette, thick borders, hard shadows, zine texture, punk editorial energy, no official party logos, no fake seals.

## Manifesto Image

Create a brutalist manifesto cover image for The Radical Libertarian. A torn-paper declaration pinned against a cream wall, black ink, red stamp accents, thick shadows, chaotic but premium editorial layout. No official logos, no readable legal text, no flags as the main subject.

## Podcast Image

Create a bold podcast cover concept for The Radical Libertarian Podcast. A black microphone shaped like a signal flare, red sound waves, cream paper background, thick black borders, punk zine energy, political philosophy meets underground radio. No platform logos, no text overlays unless requested.

## Tools Image

Create a brutalist toolkit image for interactive liberty tools: checklists, scorecards, bingo cards, and a satirical detector panel. Cream, black, red, yellow accents, thick borders, hard shadows, rebellious editorial style. No fake official forms.

## Archive Image

Create a retro archive collage for old YouTube, Spotify, and Instagram content being recovered into a website. Stacked cards, tapes, waveform snippets, paper folders, red labels, black ink, cream background, brutalist zine style. No real platform logos.
