# Social Post Prompts

Prompts for repurposing The Radical Libertarian content into social posts.

## Essay To Carousel

Turn the following essay into a 7-slide Instagram carousel in the voice of The Radical Libertarian. Make it sharp, funny, anti-bureaucratic, and useful. Use short punchy slide copy. No fake statistics. No unsupported claims. End with a CTA to read the full essay.

Slides:

1. Hook
2. The uncomfortable premise
3. What most people get wrong
4. Why it matters
5. The practical consequence
6. The exit strategy
7. CTA

## Essay To Thread

Turn this essay into a punchy social thread. Use one idea per post. Keep it sharp but coherent. Avoid calls for violence, harassment, or unsupported claims. End with a CTA to read the full essay or join the signal.

## Podcast To Clips

Turn this podcast summary into 10 short clip ideas. For each clip, provide:

- Hook
- Clip title
- 1-sentence context
- Caption
- CTA

## Quote Card Ideas

Generate 15 quote-card lines from the following essay or transcript. Keep them short, punchy, provocative, and memorable. Avoid fake quotes from real people.

## Archive Repurpose

Given this older YouTube, Spotify, or Instagram content, create:

- A cleaned-up title
- SEO description
- 5 tags
- 3 social captions
- 1 newsletter blurb
- 1 related essay idea
