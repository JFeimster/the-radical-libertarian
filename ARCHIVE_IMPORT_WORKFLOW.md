# Archive Import Workflow

## Goal

Recover old YouTube, Spotify, Instagram, and podcast material into owned-site content.

## Workflow

1. Collect source URL.
2. Record source in `data/archive-import-queue.json`.
3. Save title, description, date, platform, and notes.
4. Decide output:
   - archive entry
   - podcast episode page
   - transcript page
   - clip hooks
   - quote cards
   - essay seed
5. Publish owned page.
6. Link to newsletter and related content.

## Rule

Do not invent old episodes, dates, URLs, titles, or claims.
